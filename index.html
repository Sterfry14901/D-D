      📖 How to play

      Welcome, adventurer! Everything you need, from joining to slaying. Click any section to open it.

      🚪 Joining a game
      • Got a party code? (looks like RF-XXXXX) — type it into the GM password box on the join screen and you'll land in your friend's game automatically.
      • Otherwise, type the same Table / room code as your friends — same code = same table.
      • Pick a class, species & background and the game builds a rules-correct level-1 character for you — attacks, spells, skills, gear, everything.
      • Leave the GM password blank — players always play free.
      ⚔️ Fighting (the fun part)
      • Open the Combat tab (⚔️) and smash the big ⚔️ Attack! button — pick a target, hit Roll. The server rolls a real d20 against their real Armor Class. Nobody can fudge it.
      • Your weapon attacks appear as one-tap buttons under it — tap your sword, pick the goblin, done.
      • Advantage / Disadvantage buttons roll 2d20 and keep the best/worst, just like the book. A natural 20 doubles your damage dice. 💥
      • You can also right-click any token → ⚔️ Attack… or ❤️ Heal / Damage…
      • On the Sheet tab (📋): cast spells (slots tracked for you), roll death saves when downed, take Short/Long rests, and hit Level Up when the DM awards XP.
      🎲 Dice & rolls
      • Dice tab (🎲): tap any die, or use the presets.
      • Type /roll 2d6+3 in chat for anything custom. Save your favorites as macros.
      • Click skills, saves and attacks on your sheet — they roll themselves with your real bonuses.
      🎒 Loot, gold & shopping
      • Items come from the world: buy them at shops (Party tab shows the shop when the DM opens one), loot chests (📦 double-click) and fallen monsters (double-click a downed foe), find them in quests, or trade with other players.
      • Your sheet tracks weight — carry too much and you're slowed. Equip weapons and armor with the toggles (two hands, one suit of armor — the game enforces it).
      • Trade items or coins: Party tab → click a player → 🤝 Trade.
      🗺️ The world
      • World tab (🗺️): see the map, travel between cities on foot, horse, wagon or boat. Travel takes in-game time and can trigger road encounters — 100 different ones.
      • In a city you can visit its vendors and buy what they stock.
      • Quests tab (❗): your objectives tick off automatically as you travel and fight — rewards (XP + gold) pay straight onto your sheet.
      💬 Talking & tools
      • Say talks to the table; DM ▸ asks the AI Dungeon Master to respond. 🎙 Talk lets you speak instead of type.
      • /whisper name message sends a private message (/reply answers the last one).
      • The 🎤 voice button connects live voice chat with your table.
      • Journal tab (📓): shared notes plus People you've met — the cast of NPCs the AI DM remembers.
      • ✨ Custom Trackers (character sheet): add your own stats — Corruption, Sanity, Faction rep, anything your table invents. Give it a cap of 1–8 for clickable pips, or leave it open as a counter. Click the name to rename; they save with your character and travel with exports.
      • 🎛 Your interface, your way (button up top): untick the tabs you never use and they vanish from your panel — just for you. Save layouts as presets ("⚔️ Combat night" and "🎭 Roleplay night" come built in) and swap your whole workspace in one click.
      • 📅 Next session: check the top of the Journal tab — your DM posts when you're playing next, and you'll see a reminder each time you join.
      • 🎵 Session Playlist (Journal tab): when your DM links a Spotify or YouTube playlist, a player appears right in the Journal — the whole table listens to the same campaign soundtrack. If the DM set a ⚔️ battle mix, it takes over automatically when initiative starts.
      • 🪑 Hybrid sessions: playing in the same room as your group? Tick "I'm at the physical table" when you join (or click your name in the Players tab to flip it). Remote friends show as 🌐 — everyone plays the same campaign whether they're on the couch or across the country.
      • 📊 Luck report (Dice tab): end-of-session dice stats — everyone's rolls, natural 20s, natural 1s, best roll and average, plus 🍀 Luckiest and 🪦 Cursed verdicts. Hit 📣 to post the report to chat and settle the "the dice hate me" argument with data.
      • 🌀 Condition tooltips: hover any condition — the emoji on a token, the chips on your sheet, the strip in the token menu — and the actual 5E rule appears. No more "wait, what does Restrained do again?".
      • ⚔️ Turn helper: when your turn starts, a card pops up with what you can do — move, action options (with your actual attacks), bonus action, reaction — plus one-tap Attack / Cast / Roll buttons. Great while you're learning; hide it forever with one click, or turn it back on here.
      • 💀 Exhaustion tracker (character sheet, under Conditions): click the skull pips to set your level — the exact penalties show right next to it, stacking as they should. A long rest removes one level automatically.
      • 🌟 Inspiration: when the DM rewards your roleplay, your sheet lights up and the whole table hears about it. Spend it from your sheet — it announces "advantage on the next roll!" so nobody forgets you earned it.
      • 👁 Just watching: tick it on the join screen to spectate — see the map, follow the chat, cheer from the sidelines with no character and no turn prompts. Great for friends checking out the game before they roll one up. When you're hooked, hit the glowing 🎲 Join the adventure button and you're in — the table gets the good news and your character sheet opens.
      • 💰 Party Loot (Combat tab): when the DM drops a haul, 🖐 Claim what you want (first come!) or 🎲 Roll for contested items — winner takes it, straight onto your sheet. Split gold arrives in your coin pouch automatically.
      • ⚔️ Maneuvers (Combat tab, if your DM enables them): martial classes get Superiority Dice — spend one on Trip, Disarm, Riposte, Menace, Push or Precision Strike. The die and the save DC post to chat automatically; dice come back on a rest.
      • 🔗 Linked notes: type @ in a notebook note and pick a character — the name becomes a gold link that jumps to their card (dead ones show struck-through). Type #waystation style tags and click any tag to see every note using it. NPC cards grow a "🔗 2 notes" button showing everywhere you've written about them. Your notes become a campaign wiki without trying.
      • 📔 My Notebook (in the Journal tab): your own private session notes — nobody else sees them. Attach a 📷 photo of your real notebook pages, paste a screenshot straight into the note box, or draw a quick ✏️ sketch (monster doodles, hand-drawn maps). Notes are saved with the campaign, so they're waiting for you next session.
      • Found something broken? Hit 🐛 up top — it goes straight to the devs.
      ⚔️ Combat & dice — what's new
      • Every roll tumbles a big d20 on the battle map for the whole table to see.
      • Roll a natural 20 and chat shouts 💥 NATURAL 20! — a natural 1 gets 💀 CRITICAL FAIL. No hiding it now.
      • When combat starts, a floating tracker shows the round and turn order over the board. When you're next, you'll get a "🎯 You're on deck" heads-up — plan your move!
      • Your token's health bar sits on top of it like a video game. You can see the party's bars — monster HP is the DM's secret.
      • Double-click your token to pick a portrait — there's a gallery of knights, paladins, bards and more.

      The DM master guide — run your first session in 10 minutes, then go deep. Every tool, in the order you'll need it.

      🚀 Session zero: set up your table (2 min)
      • Join with a room code of your choosing and set a GM password — first password claims the DM seat. (New tables get 3 free DM session-days; after that it's DM Pro. Players never pay.)
      • 🚀 Setup (top bar): new campaign? The Session Zero wizard walks you through the whole thing in one pass — party code, first session time, playlists, first map, first quest. It pops up automatically on a fresh table; steps turn green as you go.
      • 💾 Backup / 📥 Restore (chat DM row): download your ENTIRE campaign — map, tokens, quests, NPCs, journal, shop, world, scenes, loot — as one file, and restore it any time. Insurance against anything; also the way to move a campaign to a new room. Player logins and private notebooks never leave the server.
      • 🌟 Award Inspiration (Players tab): hit the star next to a player's name for great roleplay — their sheet lights up, the table gets the announcement, and spending it announces itself too. The cheapest player-happiness button in the game.
      • 🌙 End session (chat DM row): the closing ritual in one tap — posts the night's dice luck report, logs "Session N complete" to the Chronicle, then walks you to the next-session banner and a backup download. End every night with it and your campaign stays organized forever.
      • 🔇 / 🚪 Moderation (Players tab): mute a disruptive player's chat (they're told, the table isn't) or remove them from the table entirely. Sits next to the 🌟 Inspiration star — carrot and stick in one row. Handy now that anyone can 👁 spectate.
      • Click 🎟️ Party code (top bar) — send the code to your players. They type it in the GM password box and land in your game as players.
      • Want a co-DM? Give them your GM password instead.
      • Try the whole game solo first: the green 🎲 instant demo button gives you a seeded adventure to poke at.
      🧠 Your AI co-DM (free!)
      • Click the 🧠 DM badge to point the AI at your own free Ollama (5-min setup — ask in Discord #help-and-questions) or leave it on the server default.
      • DM ▸ makes it narrate. It sees the battlefield, HP, dice results, quests — and every NPC in Journal → People you've met (🎭 Improv NPC adds them automatically, so it never forgets a character).
      • Improv set: 🎭 NPC · 🏰 Describe place · 🎣 Plot hook — instant content when players go off-script.
      • 🤫 Secret roll (next to the Improv set): roll any dice behind the screen — only YOU see the result. Perfect for hidden perception checks, stealth vs the party, or "rolling to see if the bridge holds". Tick 👁 tease to post "the DM rolls something behind the screen…" with no numbers — nothing kills metagaming faster.
      • 🧭 Travel event (next to the Improv set): pick the terrain — road, forest, mountains, swamp, desert, coast or underground — and one tap posts a ready-to-run travel moment to chat: encounters, discoveries, hazards, mysteries. 70 hand-written events, works fully offline (no AI needed). Great for making journeys feel alive instead of "you travel for three days".
      • 📖 Recap writes a "Previously on…" from your session log. 🔊 voices read the DM aloud.
      • AI can also: suggest quests (Quests tab), stock your shop, and generate loot straight onto a player's sheet.
      ⚔️ Running combat
      • Drop monsters: Combat tab → search the bestiary (or Open5e's thousands) → click to place a token with real HP/AC. Save whole groups as encounters and re-drop them later.
      • 📅 Next session (Journal tab): type when you're playing next ("Friday 7pm EST") + an optional note — every player sees it in their Journal and gets a reminder when they join. 📣 Announce posts it to chat. Pair it with the Sesh bot in the Discord for RSVPs, and scheduling stops being the campaign killer.
      • 🎵 Session playlist (Journal tab): paste a Spotify or YouTube playlist link and every player gets a built-in player in their Journal — your campaign soundtrack travels with the room and survives restarts. Add an optional ⚔️ combat playlist and the music auto-switches to your battle mix the moment initiative starts, then back when the fight ends. Pairs with the 🔊 ambient soundscapes for layered audio.
      • ✨ Custom Trackers: running a homebrew mechanic — corruption, sanity, faction favor? Players add it to their own sheet in seconds (character sheet → Custom Trackers) with pips or counters. Pairs with the 🧪 Homebrew Maker for fully homebrew campaigns.
      • 🎛 Custom interfaces: every player can hide tabs they don't use and save layout presets — a Fighter can run a lean ⚔️ combat layout while your bard keeps the 🎭 roleplay tabs. Each person's layout is their own; it changes nothing for anyone else.
      • 💰 Party Loot (Combat tab): type the haul ("Flame Sword, 3 Potions") plus gold and hit Drop haul. Players 🖐 Claim items first-come or 🎲 roll off (one d20 each, you 🏆 Award to the highest); claimed items land straight on their sheets. ⚖️ Split gp evenly divides the gold across the party with the remainder staying pooled. No more twenty-minute loot arguments.
      • 🎛 Optional rules (Combat tab): flip ⚔️ Martial maneuvers ON and your Fighters, Barbarians, Monks, Rogues, Rangers and Paladins gain Superiority Dice (d8s, short-rest recovery) plus six battle maneuvers — Trip, Disarm, Riposte, Menace, Push, Precision. One tap rolls the die and posts the save DC to chat. A clean fix for the martial/caster gap — and it's your call, per campaign.
      • 🧪 Homebrew Maker (Combat tab): invent your own monster — type a name, pick a CR, type and size, hit ⚒ Forge. It gets HP, AC, attack bonus and damage balanced to match official monsters of that CR, plus matching token art, and drops straight onto the map ready for the ⚔️ combat assistant. Your creations are saved for re-dropping (right-click one to delete).
      • 👥 Add party (roll init) puts everyone in initiative with rolled d20s; Next ▸ advances turns (players get a chime), the turn timer nudges slowpokes.
      • Right-click a monster → ⚔️ Attack… — its real attack profile prefills (SRD stats built in), advantage buttons, crits double dice, temp HP soaks first. The encounter meter shows Easy→Deadly as you build.
      • Whole-party tools: 💥 Damage / 💚 Heal / 🌙 Full heal everyone at once. AoE templates auto-hit tokens inside (save-for-half supported).
      • Conditions, auras, concentration and death saves are on the right-click menu. Downed players alert you automatically.
      🗺️ Maps, fog & atmosphere
      • Battle maps: pick from the built-in library or upload any image (map packs, Azgaar exports…). Adjust grid size to fit.
      • Fog of war hides the unexplored; walls + dynamic lighting give true line-of-sight per token. Brush sizes 1×1 to 5×5.
      • World tab: your overworld — upload a world map image (🖼️), pin your cities (📍), and let players travel. Each city has vendors you control.
      • Set the mood: weather overlays (rain/snow/fog/embers) + synced soundscapes (tavern/dungeon/forest…). Show any image to the table with the 🖼 handout board.
      • Hidden tokens (GM layer) are invisible to players until you reveal them — great for ambushes.
      ❗ Quests & rewards (the engine works for you)
      • Create quests with objectives like "reach Silverkeep" or "slay 3 wolves" — they complete themselves as the party travels and fights, and pay XP + gold straight onto every sheet.
      • 🧭 AI: suggest quests writes a main + side quests in one click. 🎬 Ready check asks the table, then lets everyone pick the quest together.
      • Award bonus XP any time; milestone-level the whole party with one button.
      💰 The economy (anti-cheat by design)
      • Players can't invent items — everything flows through you: the shop (open it, stock it by hand or let the AI stock it), 📦 loot chests you place, loot you stock on monsters, quest rewards, 🎁 Give item, and player↔player trades.
      • Prices, buying AND selling work automatically against their coin pouches. DM-only magic items include crafting time/cost.
      👁️ DM oversight tools
      • Party tab: live HP bars + read-only view of every character sheet. Whisper any player privately.
      • Give any token to a player (right-click → give control) — companions, familiars, that goblin they adopted.
      • ⛶ (end of the tab row) makes your panel full-screen — DM mission control. Pop-out sheet puts a sheet on your second monitor.
      💾 Never lose a campaign
      • Everything autosaves and survives server restarts — maps, tokens, world, shop, quests, NPCs, chat.
      • 💾 Download campaign (belt-and-suspenders) saves a file; load it into any room later — even on another server.
      🌟 Pro tips
      • Prep is optional here: demo room + AI improv + quest engine means you can run a session with literally zero prep. Try it.
      • Let the AI narrate flavor while you make rulings and voice the big NPCs — best of both worlds.
      • Keep the Journal open — players write in it, and "People you've met" becomes your campaign bible automatically.
      • 🔗 Linked notes: you and your players can @mention any character from People-you've-met inside notebook notes (with autocomplete) and use #tags — clicking a link jumps to the NPC card, and each card shows backlinks to every note that mentions them. 66 NPCs stays navigable.
      • ☠️ Dead/Alive NPC tags: long campaigns rack up dozens of NPCs (66 by episode 37 is real). Hit ☠️ on a card to mark someone dead — they grey out with a strikethrough, the All/Alive/Dead filter keeps the list usable, and the AI DM is explicitly told never to bring a [DEAD] character back as anything but a body, memory, or ghost. 💚 revives them if you change your mind.
      • 📔 My Notebook: every player (and you) gets a private notebook in the Journal tab — text notes, pasted photos of real notebook pages, and a built-in ✏️ sketch pad. Great for players who keep hand-drawn maps and monster doodles; everything persists with the campaign. Tell your table it exists!
      • Join the Discord (💬 on the join screen) — #dm-lounge is behind the screen, players can't see it.
